PRO vs. FREE Version

The free version of the app requires an internet connection. Once online, advertisements will show on the upper part of the screen. If you go offline, many of the features will not work.

If you want to have all of the benefits and features of the app, we recommend buying the PRO version. For a glimpse of what the app can offer, refer to the following table to see the distinctions between the free and PRO versions.
PRO FEATURES
  • Internet is not required
  • No advertisements
  • 3D graphs
  • Save your own constants, functions and ﻿expressions﻿ in your library
  • Real-world list of Physical constants
  • ﻿﻿﻿Full screen﻿ for graphs﻿﻿
  • 9 workspaces
  • Individual settings for each workspace (enabled in general settings)
  • History size of up to 100 entries
  • Entry length of up to 500 characters
  • Up to 10 graphs in 1 workspace
FREE VERSION FEATURES
  • Internet is required
  • Contains advertisements
  • No 3D graphs
  • Cannot save your own constants, functions and expressions in your library
  • Real-world list of Physical constants
  • No Full screen option for graphs
  • 3 workspaces
  • Workspaces keep the same calculator and graph settings
  • History size of up to only 10 entries
  • Entry length of up to only 100 characters
  • Up to 5 graphs in 1 workspace

            Exponent 5^x = 625 --> x = 4
            Logarithmic log (3x - 4) = 2 -->  x = 34  2/3

4.4. Changing to White Background


Both white and black backgrounds are available for graphs. The default background is black. To switch to a white background:
1) Tap the menu button on the upper left corner of your screen.
2) Tap Settings. 
3) Tap Graph.
5) Check the white background option. Uncheck the box to switch back to a black background.
